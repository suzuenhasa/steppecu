## Group 7 — Duplication

- [7.1][LOW] f2_block_kernel.cu:357-359,363-365,370-372 — The three `cublasGemmEx` calls are near-identical copy-pasted blocks differing only by a constant (m=P vs m=twoP), the matrix pointers, and the leading dimension (P vs twoP); all share the same `CUBLAS_OP_N, CUBLAS_OP_T`, `&one`/`&zero`, `CUDA_R_64F`, `ct`, `CUBLAS_GEMM_DEFAULT` tail. - [7.2][LOW] f2_block_kernel.cu:124,128 — `Q_raw[idx]` is loaded twice in the feeder kernel: once into `q` (gated by `valid`, line 124) and once passed raw into `het_correction(Q_raw[idx], ...)` (line 128).

## How to read this plan

- **Ranks 1–12** are hygiene and contract work. They have high impact because they unblock code review, onboarding, and downstream packaging.
- **Ranks 13–22** are structural and performance work. They change the shape of the code and the GPU backend.
- **Ranks 23–35** are targeted cleanups that reduce duplication, improve ergonomics, and harden the product surface.

Do not try to parallelize everything. Start with rank 1 (CI); without it, every subsequent change is harder to land safely.
